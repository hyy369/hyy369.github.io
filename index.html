My Self

I am a scientist, artist, and learner at Columbia University in the City of New York. I believe technology should communicate with humans. I always remember the striking power of inclement snowstorms, exotic creatures, and poetic space odyssey in the sci-fi movies. Computer graphics have brought artists a new dimension of creative capability. I was deeply moved when I watched Citius, Altius, Fortius, an experimental animation, and decided to come into this field.

In my works, I use simple forms and colors while experimenting new tools and technologies. For example, I tried out particle rendering effects in my first independent animation Gemini. My works range from from animated shorts and computer games to mobile games and image renderings.
